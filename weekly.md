                ## Pressure Rep Templates (10 min)

                ### Exams — Timed Retrieval
                - 6 min: answer from memory (no notes)
                - 3 min: check + correct
                - 1 min: write the **one mistake pattern**

                ### Work — OODA Note
                - Observe (facts)
                - Orient (constraints + incentives)
                - Decide (next action + fallback)
                - Act (send / schedule / write the task)

                ### Social — Calm Boundary Rep
                > “I can do X. I can’t do Y. If Y is required, we’ll need Z (time/priority/tradeoff).”

                ### Social — Incentive Map (5 min)
                - They want: ___
                - They fear: ___
                - Constraints: ___
                - Likely next move: ___
                - My best lever: ___
